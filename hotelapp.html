Features

Branded Design & Custom Navigation : Hotel App allows hotels to use their own unique branded look and offers customizable sub-navigation and flexible layouts. Your Android and iPhone hotel app will be exclusive to your property, and content can easily be updated at any time using Hotel App intuitive content management system.

Mobile Bookings : Hotel App direct access to your hotel’s booking engine that makes reserving rooms simple and convenient for guests from any location.

Things to Do : Powered by Yelp, the convenient interactive map is your personal city guide. Discover all there is to see and do, from dining, nightlife, sporting events and museums to shopping districts and attractions. Click on the map icons for details and reviews.

Recommendations : Explore the local scene as a local would. View custom itineraries and recommendations created by the hotel. Itineraries can be customized for everyone from families to solo travellers.

Push Notifications : Receive exclusive hotel promotions directly on your mobile device. Click on the notification box to launch Hotel App and get the full scoop in an instant.

QR Scanner : Access a QR scanner tool without leaving the app.

Search Property Listings : The unique needs of hotel management companies and hotel associations are addressed with the added ‘Search Property Listings’ feature. Your properties can be broken down by country, state, city or property name. Each individual property within the app includes its very own listing with a photo gallery, property information and booking options.

Property Information : Discover on-site dining venues, view special event and meeting options, and browse the hotel photo and video galleries.

Specials & Last Minute Deals : Promote targeted specials and last-minute deals to guests quickly and easily. Simply enter your deal information into the CMS, choose your recipients, and send! A great tool for increasing occupancy over your hotel’s need dates.

At Your Service : Hotel App is a personal concierge, connecting guests to the hotel’s guest relations desk at any time, day or night. Contact the hotel directly with single-click email and dialling options. Guests simply submit a request or question and select how they’d like the hotel to reply – by phone, email or text.

Get Social : Connecting directly to Facebook, Twitter and email, Hotel App allows users to share photos, messages and mobile app content with followers and friends in real time.

Multilingual : Reach guests around the globe by taking advantage of Hotel App multilingual functionality. You can load your app content in up to 10 languages.
