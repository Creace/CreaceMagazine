About the Company

Creace technologies pvt ltd, incorporated in May 2009, is promoted by eight young entrepreneurs, with the objective of developing innovative products and to provide IT services with the international quality and standards. The company was first named as Lino Computers technologies Pvt Ltd and later it was renamed as Creace Technologies Pvt Ltd at the month of July in 2011.

Vision

Creace Technologies Ltd vision is to be the pioneer in the field of new generation technology, by implementing their innovative concepts and developments in the market. The company is constantly emphasising on its new concepts and also believes in its potential to make these innovative concepts into reality.

Mission

Creace Technologies Pvt Ltd mission is to develop its innovative ideas into product and contribute these high end innovations into an ever demanding global market. The company realises the importance of technology in day to day life and always believe in performing upfront in the market.

Objectives.

The objectives of Creace Technologies are:
  • To develop new products and to bring about continuous improvement to the existing product.
  • To achieve growth in terms of size, turnover and margins at a brisk pace in the short run and sustain such momentum in the long run by taking initiatives for business expansion at appropriate intervals in order to become a high net worth company.
  • To concentrate mainly on products which attract end customers and which is also the best of marketing a good product in the market.
  • To design and develop innovative ideas and mobile technology concepts into products with highly commanding usefulness in the market.
  • To keep a close watch at the developments taking place in the mobile enterprise industry and to stay competent enough in the current market.
